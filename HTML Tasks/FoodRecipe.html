PAV BHAJI RECIPE

Ingredients

  • 3 medium size potatoes
  • ½ cup green peas
  • ¾ cup chopped cauliflower
  • ¾ cup chopped carrot
  • 3 medium size tomatoes
  • 6-8 garlic cloves
  • Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1 medium size or ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped capsicum
  • Butter Sticks
  • Cummin Seeds
  • Pinch of Asephotipda
  • Red chili powder
  • Pavbhaji masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Garam Masala
  • Lemon juice
  • Coriander leaves
  • Bowl

 Pressure cook Potatoes, Green peas, chopped cauliflower, and chopped carrots.Mash boiled vegetables with a masher.

 In a mixture jar, add chop 1 tomatoes, ginger, garlic and make a paste.Fine chop onion, 2 tomatoes and capsicum

 Now In a pan, add butter, oil, and cumin seeds. Sauté for a minute.Now add chopped onion and cook it till onion becomes translucent.

 Add chopped capsicum and sauté it for 2 minutes.Mix well.Add boiled and mashed vegetables and mix well. Cook mixture for 10 minutes.

 Add 2.5 spoon pav bhaji masala and mix well. Now add 1 glass of slightly hot water and salt. Mix well and bring it to a boil.

 Lastly add Garam masala, and lemon juice. Mix well.Switch off the gas. Sprinkle some coriander leaves and add some butter on top.

 Heat butter in a pan, add little pav bhaji masala mix well. Make a slit into the pav and roast it from both sides.Serve Pav with Pavbhaji.
